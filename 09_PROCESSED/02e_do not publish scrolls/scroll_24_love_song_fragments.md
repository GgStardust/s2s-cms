So\!  
Show me Your Sky.  
The wide expanse of I  
feeling each-other  
	in the subtly  
			of sound  
 the vibration  
	of  
		that blended SKY  
			 FLY  
   
	harmonizing  
	our   
		song.  
Harmonizing with ancient   
Song  
All is  
synced   
under our wings  
the weather  
Of our spirit.
