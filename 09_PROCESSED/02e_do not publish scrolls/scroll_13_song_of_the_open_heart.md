Song of the Open Heart  
Heart open  
the taste of alchemy on the back of my tongue  
a new way of being  
I gaze up to see something slowly falling from the sky  
as i put my hand out  
it disappears  
and I hear  
"you’re not alone   
open your heart forever"  
Hearts don’t break \- they open\!
